Srocosmu

A mile-wide portal that connects Earth and Znebo. It floats just under mile above the north pole and it is sustained by machines built by an unknown race.

Zunej

An animal that came from the other side of the portal. Their outer appearance would lead you to believe they are simply "furries", but they are actually magical in nature. They are made of a substance called "Inaci". They also have the ability to use something called "Srotsi".

Inaci

Inaci is a crystalline gel that fully liquifies at temperatures above 59F and completely solidifies at temperatures below -10F [give~take 10F]. It can manipulate itself when imbued with Srotsi, as though it were alive. Animals made of this substance are called "Acaj"* by the Zunej.

Srotsi

All Acaj and fluffy* animals have access to an aura called Srotsi. It may either be physical or enhance one’s abilities, and its limits/rules are only defined by the user’s mind and their innate Srotsari Potential.

The Srotsari Potential of an Aci or fluffy animal is divided into four levels of potential destruction to the environment surrounding them:

  • SR-I - Low to none.
  • SR-II - Low to moderate.
  • SR-III - Moderate to high.
  • SR-IV - High to extreme.

Exposure to this magic in any form incites transfurmation in non-fluffy animals. It can be stopped midway through by one's sheer willpower, but never undone.
The transfurmation can be partial, but it will always be at least half of the body.
Srotsi can be contained within objects and converted to and from any other types of energy.

Nagya, Naya

An electrically charged form of petroleum the penguins use in their expirements on humans and other animals. It will cause forced transformation on any maelu animal affected by it, fluffy or not.
An Aci will slowly lose all its Srotsi [and therefore its life] upon making contact with it. [the process is very slow, but there's no known way to stop it]
A maelu animal affected by it is able to change their form to whatever they want, though there is typically a time-limit at which they must return to their "stable form", or a condition that must be met before transformation can take place.

They landed on the ice unharmed, despite their apparent crystalline structure. Scientists were quick to try and SCP the animals, but word of their existence somehow got to the public. These animals, they were similar to ours, but more.. human. Anthropomorphic animals \ or "furries", as they would be called by the public. Not that many people really cared, since it was a minor issue what to do about them. More serious things were going on at the time. Did you see 2020?

When the furries came to earth, however, they also brought with them the ability to use magic. It was instantly granted to all animals the moment the portal opened. But those poisoned by petroleum, they would not be able to use this power without transforming akin to the furries.
Penguins used their magic to operate the abandoned technology, and they did countless experiments on various animals. We'll get back to them later, since I'm explaining in chronological order.

It was found that simply being exposed to active magic could incite transfurmation in a human. This is where things might go awry. The furries are still contained in the scientific facilities. Researchers have managed to learn a bit about them.
The furries call themselves the "Zunej". Zunej are made of a crystalline gel they call "Inaci", which freezes/melts at 15C [59F].
